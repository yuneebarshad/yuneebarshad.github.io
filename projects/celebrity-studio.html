Celebrity Studio

An influencer & content-creator management sim.

  • Progression Systems
  • UI/UX
  • 3D Isometric Art Direction
  • Unity3D
Overview

Celebrity Studio puts players in charge of growing a media empire across two fictional social platforms — TokTik (a TikTok analogue) and Instaglam (an Instagram analogue). Players manage rooms, talent, and content pipelines, upgrading each space through a full isometric progression system.

My Role

Design and technical direction end-to-end: platform branding and iconography for TokTik and Instaglam, the isometric room-upgrade art direction, and a full redesign of the game's layout — moving it from a corridor-based structure to a hub-and-zone model with branching wings. The progression system itself was structured into a spreadsheet matching an industry-reference tycoon format, with precise fields for room cleaning time, entrance fees, and stay fees.

Systems Breakdown
  • Platform branding: TokTik and Instaglam identities, icon sets, and in-world visual language.
  • Room progression: full upgrade tiers per room, in 3D isometric style.
  • Layout redesign: hub-and-zone model replacing a linear corridor structure, enabling branching wings.
  • Economy fields: Name, Number, TargetProgress, Room Cleaning Time, Entrance Fee, and Stay Fee, mirrored to an industry-reference format.

Ashen Tide

A Subnautica-inspired underwater survival game, set on a post-apocalyptic Earth.

  • Narrative Design
  • Systems Design
  • Unity3D
  • GDD Authoring
Overview

Ashen Tide takes the survival-in-the-depths tension of Subnautica and grounds it in a post-apocalyptic Earth — the world you know, flooded and changed. Players descend through five distinct ocean depth zones, each with its own pressure, light conditions, and threats, scavenging and building their way toward the surface story.

My Role

Full design ownership: narrative structure, the core survival loop, zone-by-zone progression pacing, and the complete Game Design Document used to hand the project off to a collaborating artist. This includes creature tables where every mutated species is grounded in a real marine-biology reference, keeping the horror plausible rather than arbitrary.

Depth Zones
  • Zone 1 — Shallows: light penetrates fully, introductory threats and resource gathering.
  • Zone 2 — Twilight: dropping light levels force reliance on tools and sound cues.
  • Zone 3 — Midnight: total darkness, pressure hazards begin affecting equipment.
  • Zone 4 — Abyssal: extreme pressure, the first apex predators appear.
  • Zone 5 — Trench: the story's endgame zone, tying survival mechanics to narrative payoff.

Zoo Animal Manager

A complete Unity Android zoo management simulation, built from scratch — from core gameplay loop through production monetization.

  • Unity3D
  • C#
  • Android
  • NavMesh
  • Firebase
  • AppLovin MAX
  • Unity IAP
Overview Role: Senior Game Developer — architecture & core systems

A full-scale Unity Android zoo management simulation featuring exhibit expansion, animal purchasing, animal care, visitor AI, quest progression, economy systems, and mobile monetization. Players unlock zoo areas, buy and feed animals, complete tasks, earn currency, and grow their zoo through a structured progression loop.

The project also includes production mobile integrations: Unity IAP, AppLovin MAX ads, Firebase Analytics, Remote Config, Google Play plugins, rewarded ads, interstitials, banners, and purchase/restore flows.

Gameplay Loop
Earn currency → Unlock exhibit → Buy animal → Feed & care → Complete quest → Expand zoo
Key Highlights
  • Built the complete gameplay loop from scratch in Unity.
  • Implemented animal management, exhibit unlocking, feeding, and care systems.
  • Developed AI-driven animals with hunger, survival, and food-detection behavior.
  • Created NavMesh-based visitor NPC movement and animation logic.
  • Built quest, level, shop, currency, ticket, and progression systems.

Core Systems
Exhibit & Animal Management

Handles unlocking exhibits, purchasing animals, and spawning them into the correct enclosure.

  • Loads unlock and purchase state from saved JSON on session start
  • Swaps locked-area prefabs for active arena prefabs at runtime
  • Wires each unlocked exhibit to animal spawn points, food points, and cutscene triggers
Feeding & Animal AI

A physics-based feeding mechanic paired with hunger-driven animal behavior, rather than a tap-to-feed button.

  • Projectile arc aiming and Rigidbody-based food throwing, with per-exhibit active food tracking
  • Hunger and survival values ticking down per animal, with food-target detection when food lands
  • Feed state and remaining food count persist across sessions
NPC Visitor System

Populates the zoo with visitors that make it feel alive rather than static.

  • NavMeshAgent-driven pathing between destinations
  • Animation state triggers for walk/idle transitions
  • Obstacle avoidance tuning and stuck/collision recovery
Quest, Economy & Progression

Ties currency, quests, and levels together into the loop that keeps players moving forward.

  • Quest tracking across feeding, cleaning, and exhibit-unlock tasks
  • Shop, ticket, and currency systems with a level gate on new content
  • Tutorial flow and minimap support for onboarding and navigation
Monetization & Live Ops

The production layer that turns a finished build into a live, revenue-generating app.

  • Unity IAP for in-game purchase flows
  • AppLovin MAX — rewarded, interstitial, and banner ads
  • Firebase Analytics and Remote Config for event tracking and live-tunable values
  • Google Play plugin integration for store-side services
Save System & Android Optimization

The framework layer underneath the whole game — what makes it a mobile game framework, not a prototype.

  • Persistent JSON-based save system spanning animals, coins, food, quests, levels, and exhibit unlocks
  • Device-memory-aware quality scaling — camera far clip and LOD bias tuned per device tier

Overview

ROLE: Senior Game Developer — production systems & live-ops

Internet Cafe Empire is a mobile idle tycoon game where players build, upgrade, and manage a growing internet cafe — unlocking rooms, hiring staff, collecting income, completing guided quests, and reacting to live gameplay events. I joined as senior Unity developer on an existing idle-game framework and was responsible for extending it into a production-ready, live-service title: reworking core progression, building the quest and retention systems, integrating monetization and analytics, and adding migration logic to protect existing players through major content changes.

Gameplay Loop

Earn cash→ Unlock room→ Upgrade & hire staff→ Complete quest→ Handle live event→ Expand cafe

Key Contributions

  • Reworked level 2 and level 3 progression, adding new arcade rooms, racing rooms, utilities, token loaders, and area-unlock milestones.
  • Built a level-based quest system with persistent progress tracking, guided objectives, camera focus, and runtime-injected quests for thief and feedback events.
  • Implemented rewarded video systems across upgrade bonuses, level-up rewards, daily reward doubling, cash boxes, vehicle trials, and bouncer unlocks, including rotating spawn points and cooldown handling.
  • Integrated Unity IAP with product catalog support, purchase tracking, restore-purchase flow, reward granting, permanent unlocks, and premium/no-ads logic.
  • Added Firebase Analytics across sessions, progression, ad events, IAP offers, purchases, and gameplay milestones.
  • Developed migration systems that safely map legacy save and quest data into the reworked level structure, preventing broken progression for existing players.
  • Added a daily reward calendar, character/skin selection with paid and permanent unlocks, UI polish, utility interactions, and event-driven gameplay for feedback, garbage man, and thief encounters.

Core Systems

✓ Progression & Level Rework (Script)

Reworked the game's mid-to-late progression to support new content without breaking existing player state.

  • Reworked level 2 and level 3 progression, adding arcade rooms, racing rooms, token loaders, and utility flows
  • Updated room and area unlock milestones tied to the new progression structure
✓ Quest System (Script)

Guides players toward their next useful action and keeps progress persistent across sessions.

  • Level-based quest loading with persistent progress tracking and camera focus on the active objective
  • Runtime-injected quests for live events such as thief and feedback encounters
✓ Monetization: Rewarded Video & IAP (Third-Party Integration)

Production monetization covering both ad-based and paid revenue paths.

  • Rewarded video system with rotating spawn points, cooldowns, and offers spanning cash boxes, vehicle trials, surprise rewards, and bouncer unlocks
  • Unity IAP integration with product catalog, purchase tracking, restore-purchase flow, and permanent unlock handling
  • Premium/no-ads logic and reward granting tied into both ad and purchase flows
✓ Analytics & Attribution (Third-Party Integration)

Tracks player behavior and monetization performance across the live game.

  • Firebase Analytics covering sessions, progression, ad events, IAP offers, purchases, and gameplay milestones
  • Tenjin attribution integrated alongside Firebase for install and campaign tracking
✓ Migration System (Script)

Protects existing players from broken progress whenever core level or quest structures change.

  • Maps legacy save and quest completion data into the reworked level structure
  • Prevents progression loss for existing users after level 2/3 content updates
✓ Retention & Player Experience (Script)

The layer of systems that brings players back and keeps the game feeling alive session to session.

  • Daily reward calendar with double-claim through gems or rewarded ads
  • Character/skin selection with paid and permanent unlocks
  • UI polish, utility interactions, and event-driven gameplay for feedback, garbage man, and thief encounters
